Privacy Policy for The Butt Dialer

Last Updated: January 31, 2026

TL;DR: We don't collect, store, or transmit any of your data. Everything stays on your device.

What We Collect

Nothing. We don't collect, store, or transmit any of your data.

How Your Contacts Are Used

This app accesses your contacts solely to let you select which contacts to randomly call. All contact information stays on your device. We never upload, store, or share your contacts with anyone.

Data Storage

All app data is stored locally on your device. Nothing is sent to external servers or third parties.

Permissions

This app requests permission to:

  • Read Contacts - To display your contacts for selection
  • Make Phone Calls - To dial the randomly selected contact

These permissions are used only for the app's core functionality.

Third-Party Services

This app does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

This app does not collect information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever change how the app handles data, we'll update this policy and notify users through the app.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at: thebuttdialerapp@gmail.com
